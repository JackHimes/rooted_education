Student Success

This is for students who are exploring future pathways, preparing for next steps, and building the practical skills needed to move forward with confidence.

My services focus on helping students connect their interests, strengths, and goals to meaningful educational and life opportunities.

  • Project-based learning planning
  • Scholarship searches and application support
  • University and college application guidance
  • Trade program exploration
  • Goal setting and pathway planning
  • Organization, planning, and time management
  • Building independence and life skills
  • Connecting student interests to meaningful next steps

This is supportive for:

  • Exploring possible college, trade, or career directions
  • Preparing for university or college applications
  • Interested in trade programs or hands-on career pathways
  • Looking for scholarship or funding opportunities
  • Needing help connecting their education to long-term goals
  • Interested in more personalized or project-based learning experiences
  • Struggling with organization, planning, or time management
  • Needing support with goal setting, follow-through, or independence

Student Success support is tailored to each student’s needs, interests, and stage of preparation. Some students need help exploring possibilities, while others are ready to take practical steps. I help students and families think through options, create a plan, and develop habits that support long-term success.
