How it works

Tutoring

I provide individualized tutoring based on each student’s needs, goals, and learning style. Support is available in English, Spanish, general education, and ACT/SAT preparation, with a focus on building skills, confidence, and academic growth.

Home-School Consulting

Homeschool consulting starts with a free 15-minute consultation to better understand your family’s questions and goals. From there, I offer personalized guidance in state regulations, resources, curriculum decisions, and homeschool design.

Writing Support

Writing support is customized to each learner and assignment. I can help students strengthen their writing through coaching and feedback while also supporting MLA, AP, and APA formatting and more advanced academic writing expectations.

Educational Planning

Educational planning is designed to help students and families prepare for future opportunities with purpose and clarity. Support may include project-based learning, scholarships, university applications, and trade program pathways.
