How it works

Tutoring

I provide individualized tutoring based on each student’s needs, goals, and learning style. Support is available in English, Spanish, general education, and ACT/SAT preparation, with a focus on building skills, confidence, and academic growth.

Home-School Consulting

I offer personalized guidance in state regulations, resources, curriculum decisions, and homeschool design so families can educate with clarity and confidence. My experience includes working with families navigating homeschool questions and requirements in Colorado, Indiana, and Oregon.

*If you have questions regarding another state, please inquire.

Writing Support

I strengthen students writing through coaching and feedback while also supporting MLA, AP, and APA formatting and more advanced academic writing expectations. (Helpful for applications and scholarships.)

Student Success

Designed to help students and families prepare for future opportunities with purpose and clarity. Support may include project-based learning, research, complementing applications, or exploring trade program pathways.

Customized Support

Customized learning is available for families and learners who need something individualized than a single service category. Support can be tailored to fit unique educational goals, learning needs, family circumstances, or nontraditional situations, with the goal of creating a practical plan for student success.

General Inquiry / Free 15-Minute Consultation

Every learner’s path is unique. Whether your child is in homeschool, public school, private school, or another nontraditional setting, this service is for you.

Services and pricing vary depending on your family’s goals and the type of support requested. Some families prefer a single consultation or evaluation. Others want ongoing guidance with tutoring, curriculum selection, homeschool planning, or designing an educational path. Support is individualized, and pricing will reflect the time and services needed.
