Tutoring

Personalized tutoring for students in K–12 and beyond, with support in English, Spanish, general education, and test preparation.

My goal is to help students build skills, confidence, and independence through instruction that is tailored to their individual needs and learning style.

Services Include

  • English Language Arts (K–12): beginning readers, fluency, writing, grammar, comprehension, and essay support
  • Spanish (Beginner to Intermediate): vocabulary, grammar, conversation, and class support
  • General Education (K–12): math, science, history, homework help, skill building, and academic support across subjects
  • Test Prep: ACT, SAT, GED, and HiSET

Instruction is personalized based on each student’s:

  • Age
  • Development
  • Academic goals
  • Strengths and areas of need
  • Learning style
  • Pacing and confidence level

I work with:

  • Early childhood
  • Elementary
  • Middle school
  • High school
  • Students preparing for equivalency exams
  • Adults, non-traditional students
  • Families seeking individualized academic support

Session Options

Tutoring may be available in:

  • One-on-one sessions
  • Small group sessions
  • Ongoing weekly support
  • Short-term targeted support
  • Test prep packages

Every student learns differently. Sessions are designed to meet each learner where they are, provide meaningful encouragement, and create clear goals for growth.
