Home-School Consulting

Do you have questions about homeschooling laws, curriculum choices, or how to design a learning plan that fits your child and your family? You’re not alone.

Whether you are just getting started or trying to make adjustments midyear, I offer individualized homeschool consulting to help you move forward with clarity and confidence. Consulting focuses on state regulations, resources, curriculum decisions, and homeschool design. I work with families so they can better understand their options, think through next steps, and create practical plans that align with their goals.

This includes:

  • Getting started with homeschooling
  • Understanding state regulations
  • Testing requirements and evaluations
  • Choosing curriculum
  • Changing curriculum or making adjustments midyear
  • Creating schedules, routines, and learning plans
  • Finding helpful resources and support opportunities
  • Provide structure, organization, and documentation
