Writing Support

Individualized writing support is for students who want to strengthen their writing skills, improve academic work, and grow in confidence as writers.

This includes:

  • Essay writing
  • Research papers
  • Revision and editing
  • Academic tone and structure
  • Grammar
  • Sentence and paragraph structure
  • MLA, AP, and APA formatting
  • Doctoral dissertations and manuscripts
  • Chapter editing for adult writing projects

  • Academic essays and research papers
  • Higher-level writing expectations
  • Revision and editing skills
